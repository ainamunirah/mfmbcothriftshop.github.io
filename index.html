MFMB.CO Thrift Shop is a combination the names of the owners of this shop, namely Mun, Fatin, Mashi and Byya.

Our store sells thrifted such as clothes, pants, shoes, handbags and accessories.

The phenomenon of thrifting has taken the country by storm. While "charity shops" have been around for over 60 years, over the last decade, secondhand retail has grown in popularity. It has been generally looked down upon for centuries to wear secondhand clothing, but now thrifting is really... cool. At a time when cheap clothes and quick mode contributed to the throwaway head, when stuff went out before you realised it was, after shopping is a bright light.

Vision

To attain market leadership through unmatched quality, a diverse and unique product mix, empowered employees, and the highest ethical and professional standards.
